The Arcane

"Arcane" technically means "mysterious" or "little understood".

The Arcane is basically anything magic. It can be used for good, but also for destruction. Some are born with magical talents, but they are rare.

In Piltover and Zaun, most people are not gifted with the Arcane. Instead they use Hextech: a mix of technology and Arcane, or chemtech (shimmer, etc).

Some regions hunt and kill those gifted by the arcane whilst others, like the nation of Noxus celebrate magical talents (as long as the magic is honed and is useful).

In Targon, the Arcane is used for religious purposes. There they also use the Arcane for magical rituals and traditions, but never for destruction.

An example of what can happen when the arcane is used for destruction, is what happened to the city of Icathia. A mage used the arcane to open a void rift which ultimately consumed the whole city.

TL;DR all in all, arcane is just magic.
